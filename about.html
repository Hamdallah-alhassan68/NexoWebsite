    About Nexo Girls

    Nexo Girls is a youth-led initiative under the Climiradi Roberts Foundation, a registered nonprofit in Nigeria, that promotes young women's participation in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) by equipping them with globally in-demand skills to bridge the gender gap, alleviate poverty, and challenge harmful stereotypes.

    Through four programmatic core sectors— STEAM education, mentorship & leadership, social advocacy, and economic opportunities — we work to transform gendered barriers into ladders of opportunity. We believe in the power of technology and education to transform lives, ensuring a future where every girl thrives.

    Within 3 years of operations, we have impacted over 70,000 youths across Africa and beyond through digital skills programs, storytelling labs, mentorship, and strategic partnerships. While we operate in Nigeria, Rwanda & Kenya, our programs and online platforms continue to engage girls from other regions, expanding our reach through school tours, training sessions, global awareness campaigns, etc.

    The Problems We Address

    Nexo Girls is committed to addressing critical challenges affecting girls and young women across Africa through education, mentorship, innovation, and opportunity.

    Gender Inequality in STE(A)M

    Many girls still face barriers in accessing opportunities and representation in science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics fields.

    Limited Access to Digital Skills

    Young women across Africa often lack access to digital education, technology tools, and practical tech training.

    Education Gaps

    Unequal access to quality education continues to prevent many girls from reaching their academic and career goals.

    Socioeconomic Challenges

    Poverty and economic limitations reduce opportunities for girls to grow, learn, and build sustainable futures.

    Why Nexo Girls

    At NAEXO GIRLS, we believe every girl deserves the opportunity to dream, grow, and lead with confidence. We are committed to creating a supportive space where young girls are empowered through education, mentorship, technology, and leadership opportunities that help them unlock their full potential and create lasting impact in their communities.
