    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do I need experience to volunteer?

    No. Anyone passionate about supporting girls and education is welcome to volunteer.

    Can international mentors join?

    Yes. Mentors from different countries can support virtually or during special programs.

    Is participation free?

    Most of our programs are free for participants.

    How can schools partner with Nexo Girls?

    Schools can collaborate with us for workshops, outreach programs, and mentorship activities.
