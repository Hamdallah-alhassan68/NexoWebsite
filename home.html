    Our Story

    Empowering girls through education, mentorship, innovation, and opportunities in STEAM to help them grow confidently, lead boldly, and achieve their full potential.

    Many girls grow up full of potential, yet without access to the opportunities, guidance, and support they need to thrive. Nexo Girls was created to change that story. What began as a simple idea—to create a space where girls can learn, connect, and grow—has become a movement dedicated to empowering the next generation of female leaders. Through mentorship, education, and a supportive community, Nexo Girls helps young women build confidence, discover their passions, and step boldly into their future. This is more than a platform—it’s a place where dreams are nurtured, voices are heard, and possibilities are endless.

    Why Nexo Girls

    At NAEXO GIRLS, we believe every girl deserves the opportunity to dream, grow, and lead with confidence. We are committed to creating a supportive space where young girls are empowered through education, mentorship, technology, and leadership opportunities that help them unlock their full potential and create lasting impact in their communities.

    Our Vision

    We envision a world where every girl has access to education, digital literacy, and opportunities, enabling her to drive positive change and create a more inclusive and equitable society

    Our Mission

    To empower young girls to unlock their full potential and become catalysts for the change through comprehensive education, digital literacy programs, and strategic partnerships
